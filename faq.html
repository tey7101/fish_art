About the AI

+

Our AI fish recognition is powered by machine learning! Here's how it works:

What the AI Looks For:

Learn more in our About page.

Why does my fish need to face right?

+

Our AI model was specifically trained on right-facing fish images.

This means the AI recognizes fish facing right (with the tail on the right side) most accurately. Right-facing is also a common standard in fish illustration and animation.

Quick Tip: Draw the fish head on the left, body in the middle, and tail on the right for best AI recognition!

What if the AI doesn't recognize my fish?

+

Don't worry! Here are tips to improve AI recognition:

✅ Face right: Make sure your fish faces right (tail on right side)
✅ Include essentials: Body, tail, fins, and eye are crucial
✅ Keep it simple: Clear, simple fish shapes work best
✅ Bold lines: Make your lines distinct and easy to see
✅ Right proportions: Make the tail and fins proportional to the body
✅ Look at examples: Check the community tank for inspiration

Remember: The AI recognizes various styles from realistic to cartoonish! See our step-by-step guide for more help.
