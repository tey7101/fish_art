How to Join the Community

Joining is 100% free and takes less than 2 minutes:

  1. Visit the drawing page: Click the big button below and open the drawing canvas
  2. Doodle a fish: Draw any fish you imagine! Remember: make it face right for AI recognition
  3. Watch the AI validate: Our AI analyzes your doodle in real-time and gives you a probability score
  4. Make it swim: Once validated (60%+), click "Make it Swim" and watch your fish join the global tank!
  5. Explore & vote: Check out fish from other artists, vote on your favorites, and see what's trending

No signup required to draw! Just open the page and start creating. Want to save your fish or create collections? You can optionally sign in with email later.
