Tips for Creating Weird Fish

Want your fish to stand out? Here are pro tips from our weirdest artists:

  • 🎨 Break the rules: Too many fins? Perfect! Wrong color? Even better!
  • 👽 Add unexpected elements: Wings, wheels, hats - anything goes!
  • 🌈 Go bold with colors: Why be realistic when you can be SPECTACULAR?
  • 😂 Add personality: Give your fish expression, emotion, or attitude
  • 🤝 Combine things: Mix fish with other objects, animals, or concepts
  • 🎭 Tell a story: Why is your fish weird? Give it a backstory!
  • 📐 Try abstract: Geometric, cubist, or surreal interpretations work great
